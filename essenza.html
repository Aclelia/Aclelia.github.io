Essence of nature & architecture

ESSENZA BISTROT

Essenza Bistrot is a restaurant placed in Roma that aims to combine Roman and Apulian cuisine.



Two are the key concept on which the propose is based:

• nature, represented by icons that refers both to the type of ingredients used and to the simple, traditional cuisine;

• architecture, which helps us to join the two bistrot's souls: the Roman one, represented by the Pantheon planimetry,  and the Apulian one, expressed by the organic lines of the Mediterranean majolica.

The final result is a geometric, symmetrical and repeatable monogram.
