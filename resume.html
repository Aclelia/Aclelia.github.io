I am a passionate and creative graphic designer and illustrator. I always search for new captivating scenarios to draw inspiration from. My constant goal is to learn as much as possible from every experience, so I can perpetually continue to improve my work and my skills.

Through my education as a designer and an architect, I learned how to set up a graphic project aiding myself with principles of modularity and harmony. In addition, my professional background helped me to reach a deep understanding of proper and careful work organization, setting a functional workflow and properly managing deadlines.

My curiosity and dynamic thinking help me to quickly adapt to every situation, on a human level as well as professionally.
