PAUPERFORMANCE

Mildly Poisonous is my alter ego. Born by the desire to enclose in a box all other kind of works I do that have a different dark soul, a place where I can put all my experimentations.

I’m really fascinated by the world of unreal and imaginative arts. For this reason I have chose to design a logo which was a tiny illustration rich of details, moving away from my geometric comfort zone.

I have think of “Art addict anonymous” as payoff because I’m keen on the idea to create a sort of "call for artists", inviting them to collaborate for creating something unique.
