A delicate touch

NAIL STUDIO LA PERLA

A cosmetics centre focusing on hand and foot care, La Perla makes the use of cosmetics of natural origin its mantra. Soft lines and a colour palette reminiscent of the body and nature help give the logo a simple yet modern image.

The hand, a symbol of delicacy and grace, can be combined with the script in multiple ways, customising all branding elements.


Organic lines and elegant scripts in keeping with the brand's values.
