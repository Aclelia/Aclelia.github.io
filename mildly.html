MILDLY POISONOUS

Mildly Poisonous is my alter ego. Born by the desire of enclosing in a box all of the other works I do that have a different, dark soul. It's a place where I can experiment freely.

I’m really fascinated by the world of unreal and imaginative arts. For this reason I have chosen to design a logo as a tiny illustration rich of details, moving away from the comfort zone of geometric design.

The payoff “Art addict anonymous” acts as a sort of "call for artists", inviting them to collaborate to create something new and unique.
