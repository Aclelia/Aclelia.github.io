CINZIA SPIONE

Cinzia Spione beauty salon is a company born from the creativity and perseverance of the owner, operating in the field of hair fashion for more than thirty years.


The re-brand aims to enhance the peculiarities of the company, which deals with personalized treatments aimed at identifying an image appropriate to each visage.

In addition to the creation of the logo, corporate image and social pages, the redesign of the venue was also followed, with a focus on the boutique concept introduced with the payoff.

Organic lines and elegant scripts in keeping with the brand's values.
