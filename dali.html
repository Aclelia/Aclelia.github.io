Surreal cuisine for arty food

OSTERIA DALì

Osteria Dalì is an exclusive bistro that combines the love of good food and art. The cuisine at this osteria is surreal, it plays with ingredients to create true artworks.

The identity of the new brand must express this uniqueness, and it decided to do it by using the face of one of the most famous surrealist artists, Dalì.

The logo could not be different: colourful, strong, playful! Like pieces of a puzzle, the geometric modules of the monogram and the colours can be combined to build the strong identity the client was looking for.
